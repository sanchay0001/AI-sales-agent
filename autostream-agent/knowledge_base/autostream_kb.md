# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- Price: $29/month
- Videos: 10 videos/month
- Resolution: 720p
- AI Captions: Not included
- Support: Standard support

### Pro Plan
- Price: $79/month
- Videos: Unlimited videos
- Resolution: 4K
- AI Captions: Included
- Support: 24/7 priority support

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
- No refunds after 7 days of purchase
- Refunds are only applicable within the first 7 days

### Support Policy
- 24/7 support is available only on the Pro plan
- Basic plan users get standard support during business hours

## About AutoStream
- AutoStream is a SaaS platform for automated video editing
- Designed for content creators on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok and more
- Helps creators save time with AI-powered editing tools
